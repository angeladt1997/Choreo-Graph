Stage Select User Story

From the dashboard, users will be able to click on Stage Select, which will allow users to choose the stage they are working on. There will be a select drop down that allow users to choose a stage based on their studio affiliation (down the line, these dimensions and designs will be loaded to the select menu using that info). This will automatically add the shape of the stage to the screen, and will load place- holders on the screen as if the user is seeing a piece they are in. Then, they will be able to click or un-click buttons to make the x's appear or disappear. Eventually, this component will be a part of studio and dashboard, but for the purpose of flushing it out for capstone, it will be its own component.
